Waste management: Man's hardest task

We humans tend to generate a lot of waste everywhere we go. Many cities face this problem till date. The swift accumulation of trash findes itself place in the massive landfills in many of the cities. Garbage in these landfills keeps accumulating and no one seems to care about it.

The effects of poor waste management are -

  • Creates an unpleasent living enviornment near the landfills.
  • Provides breeding ground to insects and vermin that spread harmfull diseases.
  • Can pollute water bodies if waste is disposed in it.

Trivia Time!

Wind Turbines Are Getting Taller: Modern wind turbines are now towering over 100 meters (about 330 feet) tall, with blades as long as a football field. Taller turbines capture stronger winds, making them more efficient and capable of generating more electricity than their shorter predecessors.

The waste management problem can easily be solved using sustainable technology.

  • Using sustainable technology to upcycle garbage in landfills - Sustainable technologies can be used to upscale the garbage in landfills to conserve resources and decrease pollution.
  • Composting - The bio-degradable waste in the landfills can be converted into organic fertiliser using sustainable technology.
