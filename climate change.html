Climate Change: Man's worst nightmare

Climate change is an alarming issue. It is caused when gases like CO2 and methane are released into the atmosphere due to air pollution caused by -

  • factories
  • vehicles
  • burning of fuels
These gases are greenhouse gases that trap heat in the atmosphere hence, raising the temperature, causing a drastic change in climate.

The effects of climate change are -

  • Hotter temperature on average
  • More severe storms
  • Increased drought
  • A warming, rising ocean
  • Loss of species
  • Not enough food
  • More health risks
  • Poverty and displacement

We can use sustainable technology to reduce the effects of climate changes:

  • Use electric vehicles as they release very little Greenhouse gas unlike petrol and diesel cars.
  • Capturing carbonioxide is a great method to prevent carbondioxide from entering the atmosphere and keeping the air clean.
  • To get a sustainable future, we have to work on things like precise farming and organic farming, promoting land practises, these 3 points can help alot.
